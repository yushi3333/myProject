SUMMARY

Mission

The mission of London CarDreamer will be to buy and sell a desirable mix of quality used cars and and to create a friendly atmosphere where London CarDreamer will be known for being your family used car center.

Products and Services

The products that London CarDreamer will offer are quality used cars at below market value.The services that will be offered are in-house financing provided by area banks with approved credit and a full automotive detail center that will recondition all units for sale.

Customer

London CarDreamer will be focusing on three customer profiles. One being a first-time buyer, age 16-25, next being the middle-class family looking for a second car, and third, age 50 and over low-income adults.
